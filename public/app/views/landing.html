What is Steppr?

In hopes of bringing social interaction between you and your friends, and the charitable organizations you love and support, Steppr will let users challenge their friends, and "donate" their steps to a charity of their choosing.

What platforms will be supported?

Steppr is still in early development. Only Moves is supported at this time. Steppr is completely open source and support for other major activity trackers are a high priority.
